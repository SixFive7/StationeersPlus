# Bureau Busy Responses

Canned auto-replies the bureau's ancillary systems issue when a player
sends another chat message while the assigned officer is mid-deliberation
on the prior message. Senders are deliberately NOT the active officer:
they are machines, furniture, or anonymous bureau infrastructure. The
intent is to enforce strict turn-taking between the player and the
officer model, punish impatience with absurd in-lore stalling, and never
bother the LLM with these interjections.

Format per entry: heading `## N. <Sender>`, blank line, then the body
text until the next heading. The leading `N.` number is purely
organisational and is stripped by the parser; renumbering is fine but
not required when adding entries.

Add new entries between existing ones as inspiration strikes. The pool
is selected uniformly at random per interruption.

---
