Full version history for Maintenance Bureau Plus. The newest entry also appears in `About.xml` `<ChangeLog>`.

## v0.1.0: Initial prototype
- Ambient chat listener on global chat.
- 100 curated officer personas rotating per request cycle.
- Five to fifteen conversational hoops per cycle, bounds configurable.
- Global incident-damage repair triggered on approval.
- Lander-capsule blackout wrapped around the sweep.
- Persona memory persisted across server restarts.
